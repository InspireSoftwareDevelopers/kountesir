Internet of Things



Edge Computing, Internet of Vehicles, Security and Privacy issues etc.

  • Description
  • People
  • Publications

IoT can be defined as creation of dynamic global network infrastructure, by interconnecting the physical and virtual things such as ZigBee, Global Positioning System (GPS), RFID, sensors etc. and performing ubiquitous computing through Big data processing, satellite communication, cloud computing, mobile communication, database management to provide advanced services for businesses which is expected to interconnect more than 24 billion smart devices by 2020 thereby opening up a market of more than 1.2 trillion USD.
