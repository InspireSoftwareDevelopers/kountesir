About Me

I am born on 30th Aug 1985 and have done most of school education in Gulbarga, Karnataka, India. It was during my primary school days when my teachers used to carry photo frames of Albert Einstein, Jagadish Chandra Bose, Sir Isaac Newton and many other great scientists the world has produced to our classroom and motivate us to become scientists and help society to become a better place.

  • During my school days I had visited district science centre in Gulbarga, Karnataka along with my friends and teachers, these along with many similar instances were very highly motivating for me to become a scientist, I ended up doing my bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from PDA College of Engineering, Gulbarga, India-2007.
  • The next major project work I took up was on service discovery in wireless sensor networks during my master’s studies in Computer Network Engineering from AMC Engineering College Bengaluru,India. The project was about Wireless Sensor Networks (WSNs) which were an emerging technology that had a wide perspective for future applications in ubiquitous computing and ambient intelligence.
  • Also, during my research program, I took up mathematical modelling as a basic requirement for modelling of a visual attention algorithms using computational cognitive neuroscience.

Joy of Mathematics

The rigorous and beautiful logic of Mathematics has always fascinated me. I have always been a person who love mathematics and enjoy learning it. Even in school days, I used to top in mathematics even though I was not topper in other subjects.
I have always performed exceptionally good in mathematics throughout my academic life.

I scored 125 marks out of 125 marks in mathematics during 2nd semester of Bachelor’s in engineering degree.


Joy of Programming languages

High level Programming languages like C, C++, assembly level language programming like Microprocessor 8086 programming, Microcontroller 8051 programming etc are very interesting to me. So, I always like challenges of learning a new programming language or exploring a new dimension in the programming languages I know.The Projects carried out by me in UG,PG and PhD were all based on programming.

Over the last 10 Years in Teaching Profession, I have continued my joy of Mathematics and Programming by Teaching subjects like Microprocessors, C++, Microcontrollers, Circuit Analysis, Signals & Systems and DSP to the new generation.
