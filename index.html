About Me

I completed my master’s degree in AMC College of Engineering, Bangalore, India. After My Master’s degree I joined Reva Institute of Technology and Management, (now REVA University) as lecturer, where now I am working as Associate Professor in School of Electronics and Communication, Here, I got exposed to an environment that is intensely competitive, and where research goes together with learning. I have learnt a lot over the years at REVA University and made lot of friends.
